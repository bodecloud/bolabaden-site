The netstat command displays a list of [[host|hosts]] connected to your current server. You can only [[telnet]] or [[rlogin]] to servers in the netstat list. (You can also dial into hosts if you know their phone number.)

The telehack [[host]] only has only eleven hosts listed for each user. That is because hosts in telehack are arranged in a web. Each host you can connect to has it's own netstat full of other hosts you can connect to. In this way, you can connect to any of telehack's 26,631 hosts.

netstat also displays information about the remote host. It displays an * next to any [[host]] you have login on (via running [[porthack]]) or a ! if you have root on the [[host]], hostname, organization, [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operating_system OS], and location.
