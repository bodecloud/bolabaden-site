# Can we hear colors?

Source: [Perplexity thread](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/32b5f975-2f5d-481f-a1f0-8dbbd3d7d796)

## User

Can we hear colors?

## Assistant

Yes, some people can "hear" colors due to a neurological phenomenon called **synesthesia**. Specifically, **chromesthesia** is a form of synesthesia where sounds trigger the perception of colors. For example, hearing music or everyday sounds may cause individuals to see colors or visual patterns. This condition affects about 4% of the population and is caused by increased cross-talk between different sensory regions of the brain. Each person's experience is unique, with different sounds evoking different colors for each individual.[2][3][4]
