```
Conduct a comprehensive research deep dive on [topic]. 

Please follow these guidelines:
1. Provide an overview of the topic, including its history and significance.
2. Analyze the current state of research, identifying key areas of focus and any controversies.
3. Discuss the most recent developments and breakthroughs (within the last 2 years).
4. Identify leading experts and institutions in this field.
5. Explore potential future directions and implications.
6. Cite specific academic papers, research studies, and reputable sources throughout.
```

3. The "Multi-Perspective Analysis" template has garnered praise for generating balanced, nuanced responses:

```
Provide a multi-perspective analysis on [topic/issue].

Instructions:
1. Summarize the core aspects of the topic/issue.
2. Identify and explain at least 3 distinct perspectives or viewpoints, including:
   - Main arguments and supporting evidence for each
   - Key proponents or groups associated with each perspective
   - Potential biases or limitations of each viewpoint
3. Compare and contrast the different perspectives.
4. Analyze areas of agreement and disagreement among the perspectives.
5. Discuss any emerging consensus or trends in thinking on this topic.
6. Highlight any gaps in current knowledge or areas requiring further research.
7. Conclude with a balanced summary of the current state of debate/understanding.

Use reputable academic and journalistic sources from the past 5 years where possible. Cite sources inline.
```
