1. After installing and launching AnythingLLM, the first thing you need to do is create a workspace. This is where you'll organize your documents and conduct your chats.

## Adding Documents

Once you've created a workspace:

1. Look for an option to upload or add documents to your workspace. AnythingLLM supports various document types including PDF, TXT, and DOCX.[4]

After adding documents to your workspace:
