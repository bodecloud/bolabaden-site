## Assistant

Heart rate monitors have become more versatile and user-friendly in 2024, offering options beyond traditional chest straps. Here are some modern choices:

- **Chest Straps**: Still the most accurate, with options like the Polar H10 and Garmin HRM-Pro Plus offering precise readings, memory storage, and compatibility with apps and devices. Ideal for high-intensity training and swimming.[1]
- **Arm Bands**: Comfortable alternatives like the Coros Heart Rate Monitor or Scosche Rhythm24 use optical sensors for moderate accuracy, suitable for those who dislike chest straps.[9]
- **Smartwatches/Fitness Trackers**: Devices like Fitbit Inspire 3 or Apple Watch SE provide convenience but are less accurate for intense activities. Best for casual tracking.[3][10]

For accuracy and attachment, chest straps remain the gold standard, while arm bands offer a simpler and more comfortable solution.
