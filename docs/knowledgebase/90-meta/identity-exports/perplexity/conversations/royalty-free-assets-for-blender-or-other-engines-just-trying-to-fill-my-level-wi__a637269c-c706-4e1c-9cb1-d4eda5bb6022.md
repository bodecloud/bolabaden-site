## Top Websites for Free Game Assets

1. **BlenderKit**  
   BlenderKit integrates directly into Blender and offers over 33,000 free models, materials, HDRs, and brushes. All assets are licensed for commercial and non-commercial use without limitations.[1]

2. **OpenGameArt**  
   This platform provides free 2D and 3D game assets, including textures, models, music, and sound effects. It supports open-source development and allows assets to be used in both personal and commercial projects.[2][12]

3. **Sketchfab**  
   Sketchfab hosts a wide variety of free 3D models under Creative Commons licenses. Models can be downloaded in various formats compatible with Blender and other engines.[6][14]

4. **TurboSquid**  
   Known for its extensive collection of professional-grade 3D models, TurboSquid also offers a significant number of free assets. These can be used in personal and commercial projects.[8][2]

5. **Itch.io**  
   A popular platform for indie developers, Itch.io offers a vast library of free and paid game assets, including 2D art, textures, sound effects, and 3D models. Be sure to check the license for each asset.[3][4]

6. **Unity Asset Store**  
   Although primarily designed for Unity users, the store offers many free 3D models, textures, and other game development resources that can be exported to other engines.[3][8]

7. **Poly Haven**  
   Specializing in high-quality textures and HDRIs, Poly Haven provides all its content under a CC0 license. It’s an excellent resource for environment creation in games or renders.[11]

8. **Kenney.nl**  
   Kenney offers a wide range of public domain game assets, including sprites, textures, audio files, and 3D models. These can be used freely without attribution.[9][13]

9. **Free3D**  
   This site features over 16,000 free 3D models in various formats like FBX, OBJ, and Blender files. The assets are suitable for both personal and commercial use.[7][2]

10. **Craftpix.net**  
    Craftpix specializes in both 2D and 3D game assets with a focus on high-quality designs. Their free section includes characters, backgrounds, tilesets, and more.[4][2]
