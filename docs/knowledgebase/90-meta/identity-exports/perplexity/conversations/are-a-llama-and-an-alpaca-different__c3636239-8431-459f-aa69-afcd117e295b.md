# Are a llama and an alpaca different?

Are a llama and an alpaca different?

## Assistant

Yes, llamas and alpacas are different species with several key distinctions:

- **Size**: Llamas are larger, weighing up to 400 pounds, while alpacas are smaller, around 150 pounds.[1][2]
- **Ears**: Llamas have long, banana-shaped ears, while alpacas have short, spear-shaped ears.[2][1]
- **Fiber**: Alpaca fleece is finer and softer, making it ideal for garments, while llama fiber is coarser.[3][5]
- **Personality**: Alpacas are shy herd animals, whereas llamas are more independent and often used as guard animals.[4][1]
