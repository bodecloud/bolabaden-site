### 1. Prompt Drift

One of the most significant risks in RMP is prompt drift, where the iterative process causes the AI to gradually move away from the original intent or question.

- **Cause**: Each recursive step can introduce slight deviations that compound over time.
- **Prevention**: 
  - Regularly restate the core objective in your prompts.
  - Implement checkpoints to ensure alignment with the initial goal.

### 2. Infinite Loops

RMP can sometimes lead to circular reasoning or repetitive outputs, creating an infinite loop of similar responses.

- **Mitigation**: 
  - Set clear termination conditions.
  - Implement a maximum iteration limit.
