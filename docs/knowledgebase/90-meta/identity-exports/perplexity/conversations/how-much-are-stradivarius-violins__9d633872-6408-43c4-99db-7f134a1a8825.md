## Assistant

Stradivarius violins are among the most expensive and rare instruments in the world, with prices ranging from millions of dollars for authentic Stradivari creations to much more affordable copies.

- **Original Stradivarius Violins**: Prices start in the millions, with some selling for over $15 million due to their rarity and craftsmanship.[1][3]
- **Stradivarius Copies**: These can range from $50 to $50,000 depending on the maker and quality.[2]
