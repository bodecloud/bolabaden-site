# Can you show me where to find the "bot" scope in the OAuth2 settings

Source: [Perplexity thread](https://www.perplexity.ai/search/19a81516-84af-4648-9c6e-b4122894ea3e)

## User

Can you show me where to find the "bot" scope in the OAuth2 settings

## Assistant

In the images you provided, the "bot" scope is located in the **OAuth2 URL Generator** section under the list of available scopes. It is positioned on the right-hand side, about halfway down the list. The checkbox for "bot" is clearly visible in both images.

To summarize:

1. Navigate to **OAuth2** settings in your Discord Developer Portal.
2. Scroll down to the **OAuth2 URL Generator** section.
3. Look for the "bot" scope in the list of available scopes and check the box next to it.

This will allow you to generate an OAuth2 URL with the bot scope for your application.
