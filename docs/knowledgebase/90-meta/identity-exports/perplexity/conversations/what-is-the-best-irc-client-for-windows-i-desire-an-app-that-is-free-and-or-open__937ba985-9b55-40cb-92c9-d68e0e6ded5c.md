## Assistant

Based on the search results and your requirements for a free/open source and actively developed IRC client for Windows, here are the top recommendations:

## HexChat

HexChat is widely considered one of the best IRC clients for Windows. It offers several key advantages:[1][4][5]

- Completely free and open source
- Actively developed and maintained
- Based on the popular XChat client, but with continued development
- User-friendly interface while still being feature-rich
- Supports scripting in Python and Perl
- Customizable themes and addons available

HexChat strikes a good balance between ease of use for beginners and advanced features for power users. It's available for Windows, macOS, and Linux, making it a versatile choice.[4]

## AdiIRC

AdiIRC is another excellent option for Windows users:[3]

- Free and portable
- Highly customizable with extensive settings options
- Supports themes, plugins, and scripts
- Actively developed and updated

While not as widely known as HexChat, AdiIRC offers a high degree of customization that many users appreciate.

## Quassel

Quassel is a modern, cross-platform IRC client that's well-suited for Windows:[2][5]

- Free and open source
- Unique "core" architecture allows for always-on connectivity
- Supports multiple simultaneous connections
- Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux

Quassel's core-client model can be particularly useful if you want to stay connected to IRC even when you're not actively using your computer.

## Other Options

While not as highly recommended for Windows specifically, other popular open source IRC clients include:

- Irssi: A powerful terminal-based client, popular among advanced users[6][1]
- WeeChat: Another terminal-based client known for its extensibility[5][6]
- Pidgin: A multi-protocol client that supports IRC along with other chat protocols[6]

These clients are all free, open source, and actively maintained, meeting your specified criteria. HexChat is likely the best starting point for most Windows users due to its balance of features and ease of use, but AdiIRC and Quassel are also excellent choices depending on your specific needs and preferences.
