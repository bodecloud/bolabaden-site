Denver Colorado arcade bar

## Assistant

Denver, Colorado, is home to a vibrant arcade bar scene, offering a mix of retro gaming, modern arcades, and craft beverages. Here are some of the top arcade bars in the city:

## **1. The 1UP Arcade Bar**
The 1UP Arcade Bar is one of the most popular arcade bars in Denver, with multiple locations: LoDo (1925 Blake St.), Colfax (717 E. Colfax Ave.), and Greenwood Village. It features a wide variety of classic arcade cabinets, pinball machines, and modern games. The atmosphere is lively, with affordable drinks and happy hour specials. The LoDo location is especially known for its large selection of pinball machines and outdoor games like giant Jenga and tic-tac-toe.[1][2]

## **2. Player's Pub**
Located at 8 S. Broadway, Player's Pub offers a cozy arcade bar experience with around 20 arcade games, including classics like *Big Buck Hunter* and *Ski Ball*. The bar is known for its unique infused vodkas and friendly service. It's a great spot for those looking for a laid-back vibe with both gaming and sports on TV.[3][1]

## **3. Punch Bowl Social**
Punch Bowl Social (65 Broadway) is a massive venue that combines an arcade with bowling, karaoke, ping pong, and more. It's perfect for large groups or parties, offering not only games but also a full menu of food and drinks. While some reviews mention that the food can be hit or miss, the overall experience is fun and social.[4][1]

## **4. Akihabara Arcade**
Located in Westminster (8901 Harlan St.), Akihabara Arcade offers a unique experience with its collection of Japanese arcade games and themed cocktails like the "Princess Peach" and "Kirby." The bar has a distinctive vibe that appeals to fans of Japanese culture and retro gaming.[9][1]

## **5. Monkey Barrel**
Monkey Barrel (4401 Tejon St.) in Sunnyside is known for its extensive pinball collection, craft beer selection, and live music events. It has a neighborhood-bar feel with great burgers and an inviting atmosphere.[1]

These arcade bars offer a blend of nostalgia, gaming fun, and socializing over drinks—perfect for an entertaining night out in Denver!
