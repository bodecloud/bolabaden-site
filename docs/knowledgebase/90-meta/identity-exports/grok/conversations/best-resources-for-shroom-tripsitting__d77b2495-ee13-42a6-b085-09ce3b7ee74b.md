### Top Subreddits (Prioritized – These Are the Actual Goldmines)

**r/shrooms** – 600k+ members, hyper-focused on psilocybin only.  
The pinned “First Time Guide” and dosage infographic are literally the best beginner resources on the entire internet. Read them twice.  
Bad-trip threads and tripsitting advice appear daily – real people, real time.

**r/RationalPsychonaut** – Zero spiritual fluff, only evidence-based discussion.  
Best place if you want studies, risk assessment, and people who actually read the research instead of quoting McKenna.

**r/Psychonaut** – Broader psychedelics, tons of trip reports.  
Good for seeing what can go right and (more importantly) what can go horribly wrong.

**r/Drugs** – The megathread “Psilocybin Harm Reduction” gets updated regularly and is brutally honest.  
Ask anything there; veterans will roast bad ideas instantly (which is exactly what your friend needs).

**r/TripSit** – Small but specifically for live tripsitting advice and panic calming techniques.

### Live Tripsitting / Emergency Support (Use These If Shit Hits the Fan)

- **TripSit.me** → http://chat.tripsit.me (join #tripsit channel)  
  Volunteers online 24/7 who have talked thousands of people down.  
  They will stay with you/your friend for the entire trip if needed. This is the single most important resource.

- **Fireside Project** → Dial 62-FIRESIDE (623-473-7433) or use their app.  
  Free psychedelic peer support hotline, staffed by trained people who’ve been there. Launched specifically because too many people freak out alone.

### Best Standalone Guides & Resources (All Free, All Excellent)

1. PsychonautWiki Psilocybin Mushrooms page  
   https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Psilocybin_mushrooms  
   Objective, clinical, perfect dosage chart, timeline, effects breakdown. No bullshit.

2. TripSit Psilocybin Fact Sheet  
   https://wiki.tripsit.me/wiki/Psilocybin_Mushrooms  
   Short, printable, includes exact “what to do if bad trip” steps.

3. Erowid Mushrooms Vault  
   https://erowid.org/plants/mushrooms/mushrooms.shtml  
   Thousands of real trip reports sorted by dose. Read the 3–4g reports if you want to scare yourself straight.

4. The Third Wave – Mushroom Guide (2024 update)  
   https://thethirdwave.co/psychedelics/shrooms/  
   Clean, modern, includes preparation, integration, and a very good “is this for me?” questionnaire.

5. Shroomery “First Trip” thread (ancient but still perfect)  
   https://www.shroomery.org/forums/showflat.php/Number/2030853

### Non-Negotiable Best Practices (Ignore These and You’re Asking for Trauma)

- First time: **1.5–2g dried max**. Anything higher is reckless for a newbie.  
- Lemon tek or tea if stomach issues, but dose stays the same.  
- Sober tripsitter mandatory (you, ideally, completely sober).  
- Benzodiazepine (like Valium or Xanax) on hand to kill a bad trip instantly – this is the standard abort button.  
- Zero alcohol, zero weed the first time (weed often triggers paranoia on shrooms).  
- Comfortable, familiar place. Phone off or on Do Not Disturb except for Fireside/TripSit numbers.  
- Set a timer for +6 hours so they know when it ends – this alone prevents most panic spirals.

Your worry is justified. Most bad experiences come from people thinking “it’s just mushrooms, how bad can it be?” and then jumping in at 4g in a messy apartment with questionable friends.

Send your friend the r/shrooms pinned guide and the PsychonautWiki page first. If they won’t read those, they’re not ready. Period.
