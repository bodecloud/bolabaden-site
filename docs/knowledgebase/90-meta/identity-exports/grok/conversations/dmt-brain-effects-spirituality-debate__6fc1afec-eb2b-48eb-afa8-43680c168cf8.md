- **Studies indicate potential therapeutic benefits, such as reduced depression symptoms, but these are preliminary and context-dependent.** Effects like peak mystical experiences may contribute to mental health improvements, though more rigorous trials are needed.
- **While DMT occurs endogenously in the brain, its natural role remains unclear and likely minor, possibly as a neuromodulator rather than a gateway to cosmic insights.** Claims of it enabling access to tuned-out universal aspects lack empirical support and are viewed skeptically by scientists.
- **Controversy exists around spiritual interpretations, with some users reporting entity encounters and insights, but evidence leans toward these being brain-generated illusions.** Balanced views acknowledge the subjective value for personal growth without endorsing supernatural explanations.
