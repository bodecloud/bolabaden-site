### Current Promotions for Unlimited Data Plans with Free or Heavily Discounted Phones (as of November 28, 2025)

Several U.S. wireless carriers are offering Black Friday 2025 promotions that include a free or effectively $0 smartphone when activating a new line on an unlimited data plan. These deals typically apply the discount via monthly bill credits over 24–36 months, requiring you to remain on a qualifying unlimited plan in good standing. Many also involve adding a new line, porting in a number (switching carriers), or trading in an eligible device (often in any condition). Taxes are usually due upfront on the full retail price, and a credit check is required for postpaid plans. Prepaid options generally have fewer requirements and no long-term contract.

The strongest offers for truly free phones (especially flagships) come from T-Mobile and its prepaid brand Metro, followed by Boost Mobile and AT&T. Verizon deals more frequently require a trade-in for top-tier devices. Deals are time-limited and subject to change; verify directly with the carrier for your location, credit approval, and device availability.
