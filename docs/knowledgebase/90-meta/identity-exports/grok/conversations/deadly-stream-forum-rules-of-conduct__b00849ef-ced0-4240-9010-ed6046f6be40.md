Deadly Stream maintains formal **rules of conduct** for forum members posting messages or content, as detailed in their official guidelines (last updated around 2019, with ongoing references across the site).

These rules promote a civil, respectful, and on-topic community, particularly in a modding-focused environment for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic games. Below is a structured summary of the key provisions relevant to posting messages and content:

### General Posting Habits
- Place threads and posts in appropriate forums and search for existing threads before creating new ones.
- Avoid reviving long-inactive threads without substantive contributions.
- Use specific, descriptive topic titles.
- Stay on-topic; off-topic deviations should prompt a new thread.
- When seeking help (e.g., with games or modifications), list all installed modifications.
- Personal life experiences should be posted in user blogs, not forum threads.

### Prohibited Behaviors in Messages
- **Flaming and Harassment** — Strictly forbidden. This includes insults, name-calling, offensive remarks, or systematic unwanted actions, even in disagreement. Violations typically result in a warning followed by posting restrictions or bans.
- **Spamming** — Posts must contribute meaningfully. Prohibited actions include irrelevant or derailing messages, simplistic replies (e.g., "I agree"—use reactions instead), successive posts (edit prior ones), rapid thread creation, or spam-designed topics.
- **Inappropriate Language** — Avoid excessive profanity (do not bypass the censor) and leetspeak/chatspeak. Use proper, intelligible English. Minor grammar/spelling issues are tolerated, especially for non-native speakers.
- **Bumping** — Do not post solely to revive threads, except in limited cases (e.g., unanswered help requests over two weeks old, mod incompatibilities, or meaningful updates to active projects).

### Content-Specific Rules (Uploads and Shared Material)
- **Uploads and Permissions** — Content (e.g., mods) must generally be uploaded by the original creator or with explicit, provable permission. Uploading others' work without authorization results in warnings, content removal, or permanent bans. Exceptions exist for abandonware (unavailable mods with unreachable authors for ≥2 years), subject to administrative approval.
- **Ported Content** — Allowed in uploads, but subject to removal if requested by rights holders.
- **Sale of Modifications** — Strictly prohibited. All hosted content must be free. Linking to paid mods or soliciting payment leads to immediate permanent bans. Voluntary tipping (with no expectations or benefits) is permitted under strict regulations.
- **Illegal, Obscene, or Inappropriate Material** — Do not post or link to pirated content, cracks, copyrighted scans, pornography, or material inciting violence/discrimination. Discussions of piracy or illegal activities are banned.
- **Advertisement** — Avoid promoting other sites/forums or commercial products/services in posts (signatures may include links non-commercially).

### Additional Conduct Guidelines
- **Spoilers** — Minimize major plot spoilers in public areas, as new players frequent the site; provide warnings where necessary.
- **User Responsibility** — Treat all members, especially newcomers, with respect and assistance. Assume good intent and provide help without condescension.
- **Catch-All Principle** — Exercise common sense. Moderators apply discretion to maintain community standards.

Violations are reported via the "report post" feature, and appeals for restrictions are processed within seven days. Members are solely responsible for their posts.
