### Route from DEN Airport to Wynkoop St via Train

Take the A Line commuter rail from Denver Airport Station (inside the airport terminal) direct to Union Station.  
- **Duration**: 37 minutes.  
- **Frequency**: Every 15-20 minutes.  
- **Cost**: $10 (one-way; buy via app, machine, or ticket office).  
Union Station is at 1701 Wynkoop St—dead center on Wynkoop Street in LoDo. Wynkoop St runs blocks from there; walk or hop the free MallRide shuttle for 1-2 blocks extra.  

This setup beats old bus routes (like SkyRide, which took 1+ hours with transfers and traffic). A Line opened in 2016 to cut airport commute time from 90 minutes by car in rush hour to under 40, solving delays from I-70 congestion that plagued earlier options.

### Closest Station to Wynkoop St / Coors Field

**Denver Union Station** (1701 Wynkoop St).  
- It's on Wynkoop St itself.  
- Coors Field (2001 Blake St) is 0.3 miles east—5-minute walk via Wynkoop Plaza and Blake St.  

No closer rail stop; light rail like 16th/Stout is 0.5 miles south, irrelevant here. Union Station ties commuter rail, light rail, buses, and Amtrak, making it the efficient hub over scattered old depots from Denver's 1880s rail boom. Worth your time if avoiding $30+ rideshares in traffic.
