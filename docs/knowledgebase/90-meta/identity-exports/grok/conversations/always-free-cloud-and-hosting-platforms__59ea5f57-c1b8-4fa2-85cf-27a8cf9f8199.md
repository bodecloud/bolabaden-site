#### Major Cloud Providers with Always-Free Tiers
Major cloud providers offer robust always-free tiers, catering to a wide range of use cases from compute instances to serverless functions. These platforms are essential for developers seeking to experiment with cloud infrastructure without financial commitment, though they often require credit card verification and impose usage limits.

- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**: The Always Free tier includes 750 hours per month of t2.micro or t3.micro EC2 instances, 5 GB of S3 standard storage, 1 million Lambda requests per month, and additional services like DynamoDB (25 GB storage) and SNS (1 million publishes). These resources are suitable for small-scale applications and testing, with a 12-month cap for some services, but the always-free components persist indefinitely.  
  [AWS Free Tier](https://aws.amazon.com/free/)

- **Microsoft Azure**: Azure's Always Free services include 10 web, mobile, or API apps via App Service, 1 million Functions requests per month, and Cosmos DB with 400 RU/s provisioned throughput and 5 GB storage. These offerings are designed for low-traffic applications, with no expiration, making them ideal for development and prototyping.  
  [Azure Free Account](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/)

- **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**: The Always Free tier offers 1 f1-micro Compute Engine instance per month (with 1 vCPU and 614 MB memory), 5 GB-months of Cloud Storage regional storage, and 2 million Cloud Functions invocations per month. These resources are perpetually free, subject to monthly limits, and suitable for small projects and learning.  
  [GCP Free Tier](https://cloud.google.com/free)

- **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI)**: OCI provides 2 AMD-based Compute VMs with 1/8 OCPU and 1 GB memory each, up to 4 Arm-based Ampere A1 Compute instances with 24 GB memory total, and 200 GB Block Volume storage, all always free without expiration. This is particularly generous for compute-intensive tasks, though it requires debit card verification.  
  [OCI Free Tier](https://www.oracle.com/cloud/free/)

- **IBM Cloud**: The Always Free tier includes 400,000 GB-seconds of Cloud Functions per month, 25 GB Object Storage per month, and other services like Watson APIs, with no time limit, making it suitable for serverless and storage needs.  
  [IBM Cloud Free Tier](https://www.ibm.com/cloud/free)
