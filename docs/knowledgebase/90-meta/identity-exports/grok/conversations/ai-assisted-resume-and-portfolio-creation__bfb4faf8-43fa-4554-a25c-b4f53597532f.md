### Exporting Data from LinkedIn
LinkedIn provides an official way to download your data, including messages and profile details:

1. Log in to LinkedIn on a desktop browser (not mobile).
2. Click the "Me" icon at the top, then select "Settings & Privacy."
3. Go to "Data Privacy" on the left, and under "How LinkedIn uses your data," click "Get a copy of your data."
4. Choose specific files (e.g., Messages, Profile, Connections) or select "Download larger data archive" for everything.
5. Click "Request archive"—you'll get an email with a download link (small files in minutes, full archive in ~24 hours; available for 72 hours).<grok:render card_id="67b048" card_type="citation_card" type="render_inline_citation">
