### The Super Mario Multiplayer Project: Mario Royale
In June 2019, InfernoPlus developed and released **Mario Royale**, a free browser-based battle royale game modifying levels from *Super Mario Bros.* (NES Worlds 1–3). Up to 75 players raced simultaneously to reach the end flagpole or castle, competing for limited power-ups (Mushrooms, Fire Flowers, Stars) that vanished upon collection. Indirect combat involved breaking blocks or kicking shells to hinder rivals, creating chaotic, *Tetris 99*-like multiplayer dynamics. The game used HTML5 with a Java backend and supported keyboard/gamepad controls.<grok:render card_id="13e502" card_type="citation_card" type="render_inline_citation">
