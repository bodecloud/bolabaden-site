### What is Chillio?
Chillio serves as a smart media player that unifies content from IPTV playlists (such as M3U or Xtream Codes), cloud storage services like Real-Debrid or TorBox, and even Stremio-compatible sources. Available on iOS, iPadOS, tvOS, and macOS, it emphasizes high-quality playback and intuitive navigation. The app is freemium, meaning core features are free with no ads or watermarks, while premium unlocks advanced customizations like multiview streaming.<grok:render card_id="d8b09e" card_type="citation_card" type="render_inline_citation">
<argument name="citation_id">7</argument>
