Bolt.new is rated 5/5 in a recent Medium article [The best AI tools for generating websites in 2025](https://medium.com/@cecylia.borek/the-best-ai-tools-for-generating-websites-in-2025-a828696806ab), highlighting its ability to generate source code using modern frameworks, iterative design, and a browser-based development environment. ##### Momen  
Ranked first in a blog post on alternatives to v0 [Top 5 AI App Builder Tools to Replace Vercel v0](https://momen.app/blogs/top-5-ai-app-builder-tools-vercel-v0-alternatives/), Momen is a no-code platform designed for building AI apps and agents. It features a flexible GenAI framework, multiple data sources, tool invocation, a WYSIWYG editor, and Stripe integration for monetization. Its pros include reduced development costs, lower maintenance, and faster time to market, making it ideal for startups and businesses. Pricing starts with a free tier at $0/month, with plans at $33/month (Basic) and $85/month (Pro). X posts, such as one from @Momen_HQ on June 27, 2025, with 64 views, highlight its use for building AI Legal Timeline Generators, suggesting active usage. A noted con is that it is not open source.

##### Galileo AI  
Rated 4/5 in the Medium article, Galileo AI focuses on UI design, generating designs from text prompts and exporting to Figma or source code. ##### Quest AI
